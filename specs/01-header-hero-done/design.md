# Header and hero — Design

## Status

Planning only. No implementation is authorized by this document.

## Source

Derived from section 3 of `espacio-raku-next-demo-points.md`.

## Goal

Make the first viewport readable, warm, brand-forward, and immediately actionable on desktop and mobile.

## Experience requirements

- Increase the Espacio Raku logo's visual weight without overpowering the hero image.
- Keep hero copy away from the white chair/light areas of the photograph.
- Keep a clear WhatsApp availability CTA above the fold.
- Preserve a calm, welcoming visual tone.

## Design principles

- Preserve Espacio Raku's warm, calm, direct-booking identity.
- Prefer clear factual content over marketing claims that have not been confirmed.
- Keep WhatsApp as the primary conversion path where a contact action is needed.
- Design desktop and mobile states together, including loading, missing-content, and error states where relevant.
- Use semantic, keyboard-accessible interactions and sufficient visual contrast.

## Decisions to close before implementation

- Final logo scale and position.
- Final hero copy and responsive line breaks.
- Whether contrast is achieved with positioning, overlay, text shadow, or a combination.

## Acceptance criteria

- Logo remains legible at desktop and mobile breakpoints.
- White copy never loses contrast against the photograph.
- Primary CTA is visible before scrolling and is keyboard accessible.

## Out of scope for this action point

- Production deployment and SEO rollout.
- A CMS or administrative interface unless separately approved.
- Unverified client content, external credentials, or platform integrations.
